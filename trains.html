Trains

Trains Gallery

A metro isn't a metro without the means of moving passengers from one place to another, the Paris Metro uses a variety of rolling stock, both old and new.

A MP train uses pneumatic tyres whilst a MF train uses steel wheels. Some lines support only tyre tracks, whilst others use rail. Having rolling stock for both is essential
