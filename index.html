The Metro

Europe's Densest Subway

With a system length of 214km, 302 stations and an average distance of 600m between stations, the Paris Metro takes the crown in being one of the densest subway systems in the world.

Such a design presents interesting challenges.

The Facts

  • 16 lines
  • 4.16 million daily riders
  • 1.52 billion yearly riders
  • 20 hours a day

Map View

Automated Service

Driverless Trains

Automated trains can be found on lines 1 & 14. Trains can be deployed at will from a command center to cope with peak demand. The distance between consecutive trains can become smaller and smaller as coordination is not required between drivers
