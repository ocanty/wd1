Europe's Densest Subway

With a system length of 214km, 302 stations and an average distance of 600m between stations, the Paris Metro takes the crown in being one of the densest subway systems in the world.

Such a design presents interesting challenges:

  • 16 lines
  • 4.16 million daily riders
  • 1.52 billion yearly riders
  • Open 20 hours a day
